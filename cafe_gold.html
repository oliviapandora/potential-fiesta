Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Columbia, Cafe Gold boasts the best coffee and tea in all of South Carolina. Gather with your friends, family, or the perfect book and enjoy the experience of Cafe Gold. Whether it's an espresso, Americano, matcha, or iced coffee, we've got you covered. We look forward to seeing you!

  • Hawaii
